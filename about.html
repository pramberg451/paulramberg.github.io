About

Around this website you can find many useful things about me. Over the course of my time in college, I have been learning everything from the details of design work to the design work of software.

Outside of school I am interensted in many things such as video games, strategy card games, and the usual of books and movies. Many of the projects I make involve my hobbies and I want to make many different programs on the side to continue and develop my skills.
